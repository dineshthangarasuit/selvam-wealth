Is my data safe?▾
Yes. Selvam stores all data in your browser's localStorage. Nothing sent to any server. Export JSON backups regularly.
How do I back up my data?▾
Go to Profile → Data Management → Export all data as JSON. Or connect Google Drive.
How do I add it to my iPhone home screen?▾
Open in Safari → tap Share (□↑) → "Add to Home Screen" → Add.
How do I use Google Drive backup?▾
Settings → Profile → Google Drive Backup → paste Client ID → Connect.
Can multiple people use this app?▾
Yes! Each person opens on their own device. Data is completely local and separate.
How do I track insurance?▾
Assets → Add Asset → select Insurance type.
What is Savings Rate?▾
Savings Rate = (Income − Expenses) ÷ Income × 100. Above 30% is healthy.
How do I change the language to Tamil?▾
Settings → Language → tap 🇮🇳 தமிழ் button. The app switches instantly.
